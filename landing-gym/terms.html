1What you buy

The Hydropark app and its local model are free, and two skills are free (Kitchen Timer & Units, and Packing List). Each additional skill is a one-time $5 purchase. Themed bundles are offered — a three-pack at about $12 and a starter five-pack at about $18. Prices are shown in US dollars; your local price and any tax are shown at checkout. There is no subscription, no seats, and no usage metering — the work runs on your processor.

2Own what you buy

A purchased skill is a perpetual entitlement: yours to use offline, forever, the way a book is yours. You may install a skill on up to five of your own devices, and uninstall and reinstall it for free. Licenses verify against a signature already on your device — there is no expiry and no periodic phone-home that could switch a skill off. Turn off the network and everything you own keeps working.

3The offline-forever commitment (business continuity)

The promise that you own what you buy has to survive us. Today, re-download, restore, and new-device activation depend on our servers — so we commit, in these Terms, to what happens to everything you own if Hydropark ever shuts down (SPEC §28.2):

Installed skills always keep working

A skill you have installed verifies its license offline, using keys already on your device. No server is ever needed to keep using what is installed, during normal operation or after a wind-down.

A wind-down never ships a "keygen"

Our private signing key — the one that signs paid licenses every installed app trusts — is never released and never embedded in any tool, because releasing it would forge the entire catalog. Instead, before any shutdown, while keys are still under control, we batch-mint perpetual per-device licenses for every owner's every entitlement and ship them in a downloadable final offline bundle — your owned skill packages plus those pre-signed licenses. The bundled continuity tool verifies and installs; it never signs. Only public verification keys are ever published.

If new-device activation is still needed afterwards

Only then, activation uses a dedicated single-purpose "sunset" key, pushed into the trusted set by a final signed app update and released only as a deliberate "the catalog is now free" act — never a key that ever signed a paid license.

This is a trust feature, not a legal footnote: it is what makes "own what you buy" true even in the worst case.

4Refunds

Every skill carries a 14-day, no-questions refund. Request it from the account portal (PLACEHOLDER) or by emailing hello@hydropark.app within 14 days of purchase.

5Revocation and the offline promise

A refund, chargeback, or confirmed fraud ends the corresponding entitlement, but that takes effect only on the next successful online contact — a device that stays offline keeps the skill until then. The one exception that may override the offline-forever guarantee is a safety hard-block on a withdrawn skill (for example, a genuine safety issue); otherwise a withdrawn or deprecated skill you already own keeps working.

6AI-generated advice and disclaimers

Skills run a small AI model that can be wrong. Content they generate — recipes, nutrition figures, home-repair and maintenance guidance, and the like — is informational only and is not medical, dietary, food-safety, legal, or professional advice. Verify anything that matters.

In particular: the Cooking Assistant makes no authoritative allergen or food-safety claims; allergen warnings are produced by a separate deterministic rule-based layer, and you must verify allergens and quantities yourself. Nutrition Coach is informational only and is not medical guidance. Regulated-advice categories are scope-limited, and medical skills are excluded from this version.
